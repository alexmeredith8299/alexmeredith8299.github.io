Hi, I'm **Alex Meredith**! I'm a third-year PhD student in the [Aerospace Controls Laboratory](https://acl.mit.edu/) at MIT working with [Jonathan How](http://www.mit.edu/people/jhow/). I'm funded by the NSF GRFP and a Draper fellowship.

My research interests include robust control of nonlinear systems, adaptive control, safe control, and safe learning.

Previously, I completed my master's degree in the [Space Telecommunications, Astronomy, and Radiation (STAR) Lab](https://starlab.mit.edu/) at MIT, supervised by [Kerri Cahoy](https://aeroastro.mit.edu/people/kerri-cahoy/).

In my free time, I enjoy rock climbing, hiking, and biking around Cambridge, MA.
